Activity Section:

 You know how little things in life matter the most? Similarly, small positive changes in our perspective and attitude can help us go long way.
Let's see how we helped people change their attitude and perspective towards themselves.
We conducted three activities for our crowd under the event named as #celebrateyourself .

1st Activity:

" Look yourself in the mirror and don't be afraid to notice how amazing you are "
Take a mirror and try to notice 14 positive things about your face and list those 14 things
Purpose of this activity: Many people tend to avoid looking at themselves for more than a quick glance. They don‚Äôt want to activate their own critical thoughts about their appearance. We are socialized to compare our physical image with ideals and standards

2nd Activity:

Write 5 things on how you see yourself in the future.
It's now time to completely look at yourself in the mirror and visualise yourself after 5 years.
Purpose of this activity: You need to understand how many efforts you are taking in present to build a future that you expect. This will help you a lot with your confidence in future and give you clarity.

3rd Activity:

Write a letter to yourself, not as you but as someone else.
What would you say to yourself as a third person?
This might be an excellent way to Lift up your mood and #celebrateyourself.üòÉ
Purpose of this activity: The purpose is to unclutter our mind and treat ourself the same way we treat others.
